The Return of D-Nice

Wednesday, July 12 at 8 PM
Stern Auditorium / Perelman Stage

Club Quarantine Live with D-Nice

Special guests to be announced

Less than one year after his sold-out Carnegie Hall debut in August 2022, legendary artist and DJ D-Nice returns to the Hall with huge demand for another Club Quarantine Live. Throughout the isolation of the pandemic, D-Nice’s revolutionary virtual Club Quarantine brought a worldwide community of millions together, and the party has since taken on a life of its own with high-profile live events that feature unbelievable guest artists.

Club Quarantine Live is a celebration of music, life, and love, and all are invited. Wear your finest all-white attire!
